Blob Tracking Test

This version tracks motion blobs instead of faces. Any moving object, hand, body, or shifting shape inside the frame can generate a tracked region. Each detected blob receives a white rectangle and a red tracking number.

This interactive test does not collect, upload, or store your camera data. All tracking is processed locally in your browser only.

Use localhost or a secure hosted site for camera access. On mobile, the front camera is preferred first.

About

Taeyeoun Park is a digital effects artist working primarily in Houdini. His practice focuses on procedural systems, simulation, and cinematic image making, translating structure and physical behaviour into visual form. With a background in industrial design, he approaches digital work through form, material, and logic, creating images that feel technically precise and atmospherically grounded.

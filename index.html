About

PROFILE

I am a digital effects artist working mainly in Houdini, simulation, and compositing. My work is focused on building images with atmosphere, weight, and a strong visual mood. I am interested in procedural workflows and cinematic image making, and I like creating work that feels both technically precise and visually grounded.

With a background in both digital effects and design, I approach projects with attention to structure, detail, and overall form. I enjoy developing visuals that are not only functional in process, but also clear in emotion and presence.

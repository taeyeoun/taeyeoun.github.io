ABOUT

Taeyeoun Park is a digital effects artist working primarily in Houdini. His work focuses on procedural systems, simulation, and cinematic image making, translating structure and physical behaviour into visual form. With a background in industrial design, he approaches digital work through form, material, and logic, creating images that feel both technically precise and atmospherically grounded.

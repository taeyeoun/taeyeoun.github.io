About

Taeyeoun Park is a digital effects artist working across Houdini, simulation, compositing, and image making. His practice focuses on cinematic atmosphere, procedural workflows, and visually driven storytelling. With a background in both digital effects and design, he explores ways of building images that feel technically precise while remaining emotionally resonant.
